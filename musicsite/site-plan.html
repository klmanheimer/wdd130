Content

Home page- General Information

The home page will have a general understanding of what the whole website is about. It will conisist of pictures and text. Briefly describing the history and the impact music has on people and myself included. Overall, the home page will be welcoming and encourage the viewer to explore more about musics history, my personal favorites, and its emotional impact.

Images for the Home page

History

Page two is about the history of music. Specifically, how music began and how it changed over time, and why it still matters today. Starting from the early instuments. Like wood, stone, bones, and eventually to music we listen to today. Music has always been a way people experess their emotions and feelings. Page two will explore how music developed in different cultures around the world. The pictures will give visual information on instruments, musicians and the evolution of genres. Overall, this page will showhow music began and, how it has changed over time, and why it still matters today.

Images for the Page 2

Impact mentally and my personal preferance

Page 3 is about my personal connection to music. For me music is a way of me expressing my feelings and emotions. I will also talk about people who find that connection and find relief when listening or playing music. I will include a small portion of my favorite genres. The pictures will give a visual representation on how music can feel like to others, myslef included.
